# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Introduction
This project analyzes business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, profitable categories, and regional sales performance. The analysis helps businesses make data-driven decisions and improve profitability.

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset used is the Sample Superstore dataset containing:
- Sales information
- Product categories
- Regional sales
- Profit details
- Customer order information

Main columns used:
- Sales
- Profit
- Category
- Region
- Product Name
- Order Date

---

## Data Cleaning
The following preprocessing steps were performed:
- Loaded dataset using pandas
- Checked for null values
- Removed duplicate records
- Converted order dates into datetime format

---

## Sales Analysis

### Total Sales and Profit
The total sales and profit were calculated to understand overall business performance.

### Top Selling Products
Top 10 products with highest sales were identified.

### Regional Performance
Sales distribution across regions was analyzed.

### Category-wise Profit
Profit generated by each product category was compared.

### Monthly Sales Trends
Monthly sales trends were analyzed to identify peak business periods.

---

## Charts
The following visualizations were created:
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Category
- Monthly Sales Trend

---

## Key Insights
1. Technology category generated the highest revenue.
2. West region showed the strongest sales performance.
3. Some furniture products generated losses despite high sales.
4. Sales increased during year-end months.
5. Top-selling products contributed significantly to total revenue.

---

## Recommendations
1. Focus more on high-profit technology products.
2. Improve marketing in weaker regions.
3. Reduce losses from underperforming furniture products.
4. Increase inventory during peak sales months.
5. Use customer purchasing trends for targeted promotions.

---

## Conclusion
This analysis provided valuable insights into sales performance, product profitability, and regional trends. The project demonstrates how data analytics can support business decision-making and improve operational strategies.
